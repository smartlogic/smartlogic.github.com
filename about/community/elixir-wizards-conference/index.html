Elixir Wizards |> Conference

An online conference bringing together the Elixir language community; June 16-17 2021.

>>> Call for Proposals - open through April 18th <<<

SmartLogic’s Elixir Wizards |> Conference is an Elixir Phoenix community event that will take place online over two half-days, on June 16-17 from 12-4PM EDT.

Like our podcast, the Elixir Wizards |> Conference focuses on bringing together people from across the Elixir community. A few of the topics we hope to cover in this first iteration of the Conference include:

  • Elixir as a competitive advantage
  • Nx and Machine Learning with Elixir
  • Leading in Elixir
  • Hiring and training
  • The business case for Elixir
  • LiveView in production
  • Learning Elixir
  • Elixir in production
  • Elevating new voices in the Elixir community

We’re planning a mix of presentation formats, including a couple of keynotes, leadership panel discussions, short format talks, and of course, some quality social time.

Interested in presenting?

We'd love to hear from you! We are taking submissions for individual presenters as well as two panels. Submissions are open through April 18th, 2021.

  • Call for Panelists - Two panels: Women Leading in Elixir; Building Companies with Elixir
  • Call for Speakers - Short (10min) and Medium (20min) format talks
Submit your talk ideas and stay tuned for more information to be announced soon!
